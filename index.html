Reptile animal shelter

Here are the animals we have available for adoption:

Aurum

Aurum is a gold dust day gecko. He is 5 inches long and 3 years old. For gold dust day geckos, it is best if you do not try to handle them, as they are rather nervous and easily lose their tails. You will need a vivarium of at least 10 gallons. Put live plants with plenty of leaves in. Small crickets are their main meal.

Callisto

Callisto is a female leopard gecko. Leopard geckos are one of the easiest lizards for a beginner.

Leo

Leo is a male crested gecko. He should not be handled too often, and be careful because crested geckos do not grow their tails back.

Luna

Luna is a female blue tongued skink. She is used to humans and can even be affectionate. She loves being scratched on top of her head.

Sol

Sol is a male bearded dragon. He is fine with being handled.
